# Refugees data

Top 10 countries/territories of origin (excluding "Various") for period
2003-13 of UNHCR statistics on "Persons recognized as refugees under the
1951 UN Convention/1967 Protocol, the 1969 OAU Convention, in accordance
with the UNHCR Statute, persons granted a complementary form of
protection and those granted temporary protection."

## Format

Data frame with the following columns:

- country:

  Country or territory of origin

- year:

  Year (2003-13)

- refugees:

  Persons recognized as refugees under the 1951 UN Convention, etc..
